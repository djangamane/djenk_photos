ABOUT ME

DIGITAL VISIONARY & PHOTOGRAPHER

I'm Alex Rook, a cyberpunk photographer capturing the intersection of humanity and technology in our rapidly evolving digital landscape. With a background in both traditional photography and digital art, I create images that blur the line between reality and simulation.

My work has been featured in Wired, The Verge, and Digital Arts magazines. I've collaborated with tech giants and underground cyber fashion designers to document the aesthetic of our digital age.
